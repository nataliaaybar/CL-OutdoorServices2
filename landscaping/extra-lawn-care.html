Extra Lawn Care

Deeper Care for a Healthier Lawn

A great-looking lawn takes more than just mowing. Our extra lawn care services target the root-level health of your grass — thickening thin areas, improving soil quality, and giving your lawn what it needs to thrive season after season.

◐

Overseeding

We spread new grass seed over existing turf to fill in bare or thin spots, improving density and giving your lawn a fuller, greener appearance.

◐

Dethatching

We remove the layer of dead grass, roots, and debris that builds up between the soil and living grass — allowing water, air, and nutrients to reach the roots more effectively.

◐

Aerating

Core aeration loosens compacted soil by pulling small plugs from the ground, improving drainage and giving grassroots room to grow deeper and stronger.

◐

Fertilizing

We apply the right fertilizer at the right time to feed your lawn the nutrients it needs for thick, green, healthy growth throughout the season.
