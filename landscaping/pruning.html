Pruning

Shaping & Trimming for a Polished Landscape

Overgrown shrubs and plants can make even a well-kept yard look unkempt. Our pruning service keeps everything shaped, controlled, and healthy — giving your landscape a clean, maintained look year-round.

◐

Shrub Trimming

We trim and shape shrubs to maintain clean lines and prevent overgrowth that crowds walkways, windows, and other plantings.

◐

Plant Trimming

Ornamental plants and perennials are carefully trimmed to encourage healthy growth and keep your beds looking neat and intentional.

◐

Clean, Finished Look

Every pruning visit ends with clippings cleaned up and removed, leaving your property looking sharp from the street.
